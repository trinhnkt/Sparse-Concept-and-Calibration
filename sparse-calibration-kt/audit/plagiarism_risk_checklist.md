## Missing Citation Checks
- **Section 2.2 (Graph KT):** The claims about "Graph and self-supervised KT models require highly reliable evaluation protocols to prevent leakage" do not cite any specific Graph KT paper suffering from leakage. Consider citing a reproducibility study specifically addressing Graph KT, or merging this claim with the general leakage citations (`kapoor2023leakage`).
- **Section 3.3 (Bucket Thresholds):** The thresholds $\{20, 100, 500\}$ are presented as pre-registered or standard, but no prior work is cited to justify these specific integer boundaries. If they are purely empirical choices for this paper, explicitly state that they are heuristically defined for these datasets to avoid readers assuming they are an industry standard.
