\subsection{Baselines}
The minimal baseline set includes BKT, DKT, and either simpleKT or AKT. Additional baselines such as DKVMN or sparseKT may be added in an extended version. Hyperparameters should follow benchmark defaults unless explicitly justified. Model selection must use validation data only.
